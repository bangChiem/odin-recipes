Recipe Pho

Description

Pho is a popular Vietnamese dish savory and delicious. It is made primarily with beef broth and rice noodles.

Ingredients

  • Marrow bones and Meaty bones
  • Thinly sliced raw meat
  • Yellow onions
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil meat in a pot for 5 minutes
  2. skim out the impurities on top of pot
  3. add noodles to pot
  4. simmer for 3 hours bones,beef,water,salt,fish sause, and yellow onions
  5. strain pot into bowl
  6. serve!
