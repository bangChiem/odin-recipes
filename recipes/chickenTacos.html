Chicken Tacos

Description

Soft shelled delicous tacos for any occasion. Very easy to cook and very tasty.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup chicken broth
  2. 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  3. 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  4. 3 soft tortillas
  5. 1/2 red onion
  6. 1/2 tomato
  7. 1/2 lettuce

Steps

  • Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  • Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  • Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
  • Shred lettuce, chop tomatoes, and thinly slice red onions
  • Spoon into warmed corn tortillas. Garnish with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and thinly sliced red onions, if desired.
